About this room

Step inside the Seafront Deluxe and let the horizon do the talking. A wall of glass frames the sea from the moment you wake — morning light rippling across the water, evenings bleeding gold into the sky. The king bed faces the view deliberately; sleep and the sea become the same thing.

The bathroom continues the quiet luxury: a rain shower overhead, a freestanding bathtub positioned to look out at the water, and Portuguese stone tiles that stay cool underfoot. Step through the glass doors to your private balcony — the sound of the waves is constant. A breakfast basket arrives each morning before you're ready to move.
